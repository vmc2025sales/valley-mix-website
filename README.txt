LOGO
----
Place your REAL logo file at: /assets/vmc-logo.png
(Keep the same filename). PNG with transparent background recommended.

WATERMARK
---------
A faint watermark of your logo is applied site‑wide. It uses /assets/vmc-logo.png if present,
otherwise it falls back to /assets/logo.svg.

PHOTOS
------
Replace placeholder photos in /assets/projects/ and /assets/trucks/ with your real images.
Keep the same filenames to swap seamlessly.
