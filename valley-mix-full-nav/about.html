Who We Are

Valley Mix Concrete is proudly owned and operated by RGV locals. We started with a single truck and a simple promise: deliver fresh, on-site concrete with honesty, fair pricing and reliable scheduling. Today we serve homeowners, builders and businesses across the Rio Grande Valley and South Texas—still guided by the values we grew up with here.

Because we mix on site with volumetric technology, you get the exact yards you need, the PSI you specify, and a crew that treats your project like our own. If another licensed supplier gives you a better written quote, we’ll beat it.
